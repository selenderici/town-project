Most fascinating fun facts

There are 1.2 million bikes in the city. That's 1,5 bikes per inhabitant.

Has more canals than Venice and more bridges than Paris.

The most museums per square meter of all cities in the world.

Must see in Amsterdam

Dancing Houses

A row of seven houses that are slightly sunken and lean at various angles @Amstel 106.

Bloemenmarkt

The only floating flower market in the world since 1862.

Negen Straatjes

A neighbourhood that comprises nine side streets, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Top museums you'll want to visit

Van Gogh Museum

Holds the largest collection of artworks by Vincent van Gogh (1853-1890).

Anne Frank House

Where Anne Frank lived in hiding with her family for more than two years during World War II.

Rijksmuseum

The national museum, dedicated to Dutch arts and history. Holds 1 million objects from the years 1200-2000.

Best of restaurants (some of my favourites)

NENI Amsterdam

Eastern Mediterranean kitchen, owned by the Molcho family from Tel Aviv.

Parakeet

Handsome nachos and lovely cocktails

Lombardo's

The best burgers in town with juicy meat and tasty breads.
